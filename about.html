About Me | Oisin Murphy

Hi, I'm Oisín Murphy, a landscape designer from County Cork, Ireland. I studied Environmental Design at University College Cork and have over eight years of experience in creating outdoor spaces that combine beauty and function.

I’ve worked with more than 85 clients on over 120 projects — from modern gardens and rooftop terraces to wild natural spaces and peaceful courtyards. My design style is intuitive, balanced, and inspired by nature.

I’ve completed courses in sustainable planting, lighting design, and 3D landscape modelling. I believe good design doesn’t just look beautiful — it grows with you and becomes part of your everyday life.

What matters most to me is how a space feels. I believe that good design doesn’t just look beautiful — it grows with you. It adapts, breathes, and becomes part of your everyday life. That’s the kind of design I strive to create.
